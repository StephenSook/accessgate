# Netflix Timed Text Style Guide — Key Numbers (English)
# Source: Netflix Partner Help Center, re-verified July 2026
# Used as structured reference for the RAG layer and rule registry

## Reading Speed
- Adult programs: 20 characters per second (CPS) maximum
- Children's programs: 17 characters per second (CPS) maximum
- Note: The older WPM framing was removed in a TTSG revision; cite CPS only

## Line Format
- Maximum 42 characters per line
  (Relative to video resolution and ability to fit 42 characters across screen)
- Maximum 2 lines per subtitle event (prefer 1 line)

## Duration
- Minimum duration: 5/6 (five-sixths) of a second per subtitle event
  (e.g. 20 frames at 24fps)
- Maximum duration: 7 seconds per subtitle event
- Minimum gap between events: 2 frames

## Language
- English

## Rule IDs Mapped to These Numbers
- NFLX-CPS-01: 20 CPS adult / 17 CPS children
- NFLX-LEN-01: 42 characters per line, 2 lines maximum
- NFLX-DUR-01: minimum 5/6 second to 7 seconds maximum; 2-frame gap
